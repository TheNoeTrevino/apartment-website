Services

Services offered at TRECA Apartments include:

Traditional Maintenence

At TRECA Apartments, we offer comprehensive maintenance services to ensure your living experience is as comfortable and worry-free as possible. Our maintenance team is available 24/7 to address any issues that may arise in your apartment. Whether it's a leaky faucet, a malfunctioning appliance, or a broken window, our skilled technicians are ready to provide quick and efficient solutions. We prioritize your safety and comfort, and our goal is to resolve any maintenance requests promptly and professionally.

Garden Care

Our garden care services ensure that the communal green spaces around TRECA Apartments are beautifully maintained. We take pride in our landscaping, providing a serene and aesthetically pleasing environment for all our residents to enjoy. Our gardening team regularly trims, waters, and tends to the plants and flowers, creating a lush and welcoming outdoor space.

Lawn Care

Lawn care is a top priority at TRECA Apartments. Our lawn care services include regular mowing, fertilizing, and pest control to keep the lawns looking green and healthy. We believe that well-maintained lawns enhance the overall appeal of our apartment complex and provide a great place for residents to relax and socialize.

Additional Maintenence Services

In addition to general apartment maintenance, we offer a variety of other services to keep your living environment in top condition. These include:

  • Regular inspections and preventive maintenance
  • HVAC system maintenance and repairs
  • Electrical and plumbing services
  • Pest control and extermination
  • Common area cleaning and upkeep
